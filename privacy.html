Privacy Policy for Rest Your Eyes

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. Rest Your Eyes is designed with privacy in mind. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store any personal information.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior.
  • We do not share or sell data with third parties.
  • We do not use advertising or analytics frameworks.

How the App Works

  • The app runs entirely on your device.
  • Timers, reminders, and settings are stored locally and never leave your device.
  • Notifications are scheduled and delivered locally by your operating system.

Internet Access

  • The app does not require an internet connection.
  • No data is transmitted to any server.

Children’s Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, the app is suitable for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices ever change, we will update this privacy policy accordingly.
